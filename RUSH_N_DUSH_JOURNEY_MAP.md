## Journey Map Structure

The journey map is organized into **7 phases**, each with sticky notes representing:

- **Blue notes**: Phase headers and key insights
- **Green notes**: Current touchpoints and processes
- **Red notes**: Pain points and problems being solved
- **Yellow notes**: AI automation opportunities

### Phase 1: Pre-Acquisition / Lead Discovery
**Problem**: Random calling without qualification - wasting time on unqualified leads

**Current State**:
- BatchLeads import
- ATTOM data enrichment
- Web intake forms
- Direct marketing responses

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI-powered lead scoring
- Automatic property enrichment
- Predictive motivation analysis

### Phase 2: Initial Contact
**Problem**: Time-intensive manual dialing, inconsistent messaging, no conversation intelligence

**Current State**:
- Manual phone calls
- SMS outreach
- Voicemail drops
- Email campaigns

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI voice agents for first contact
- Automatic call transcription
- Sentiment analysis

### Phase 3: Qualification / Understanding Seller Motivation
**Problem**: Manual note-taking during calls, inconsistent data capture, no structured scoring

**Current State**:
- Property condition assessment
- Timeline to sell
- Reason for selling
- Price expectations
- Competing offers

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI conversation summaries
- Automatic CRM updates
- Real-time motivation scoring

### Phase 4: Nurture / Follow-up Campaign
**Problem**: Leads slip through cracks, no systematic follow-up, unclear next-best-action

**Current State**:
- Manual calendar reminders
- Spreadsheet tracking
- Ad-hoc follow-up calls
- Email check-ins

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI-scheduled follow-up
- Automated nurture campaigns
- Context-aware outreach

### Phase 5: Appointment / Property Visit Coordination
**Problem**: Scheduling friction, double-bookings, missed appointments, manual travel time calculation

**Current State**:
- Back-and-forth scheduling
- Manual calendar blocking
- Confirmation calls/SMS
- Route planning

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI scheduling assistant
- Automatic calendar sync
- Smart route optimization
- Predictive no-show alerts

### Phase 6: Offer / Proposal & Negotiation
**Problem**: Time-intensive comps research, inconsistent offer formulas, slow turnaround time

**Current State**:
- Manual offer calculation
- Property valuation research
- Comparable analysis
- Counter-offer discussion

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI-powered ARV estimation
- Automatic offer generation
- Market trend analysis

### Phase 7: Closing / Contract to Funding
**Problem**: Manual document prep, multiple parties coordination, status tracking gaps

**Current State**:
- DocuSign/paperwork
- Title/escrow coordination
- Attorney review
- Funding wire

**AI Opportunities**:
- AI document generation
- Automated status updates
- Predictive close date

## Key Insights (Cross-Cutting Concerns)

### Human Time Should Focus On:
- High-value conversations
- Relationship building
- Complex negotiations
- Deal strategy

### AI Should Handle:
- Initial contact
- Data capture
- Follow-up reminders
- Property research
- Scheduling
- Documentation

### Critical Success Factor: Conversation Intelligence
- Every interaction logged
- Motivation tracked over time
- Context preserved for humans
